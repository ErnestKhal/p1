Wealth management - income management (also known as wealth management) is a complex process, the purpose of which is to preserve and increase wealth through investing in long-term assets. it includes interrelated activities such as risk management, estate planning, analysis and prioritization of investment opportunities, estate planning, and monitoring and subsequent adjustment of investment decisions.
